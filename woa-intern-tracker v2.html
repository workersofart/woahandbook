One-time setup — 2 minutes

Do this once. Share the link with your team — they auto-connect instantly.
  1. Go to console.firebase.google.com → create a free project.
  2. In the sidebar: Build → Realtime Database → Create database.
  3. Choose Start in test mode.
  4. Copy the URL shown and paste above. Click Save & connect.
  5. Click 📋 Copy shareable link and send to your team. Done.
